About us

The Prefects' Guild is the leadership core of our school community. We champion student voice, academic excellence, and a legacy of respect, service, and discipline.

Since 1952, our prefects have led by example across classrooms, sports fields, and campus life. We mentor younger students, coordinate key school events, and help preserve traditions while bringing fresh energy to every year.

Our mission is to develop strong character, foster collaboration, and empower every prefect to become a better leader, role model, and ambassador for the school.
