About the Event

More Than a Ceremony

ROAR is the Annual Prefects' Day of Dharmapala Vidyalaya, Pannipitiya — a evening dedicated to celebrating leadership, discipline, unity and service. Organised entirely by the Prefects' Guild, it recognises the quiet dedication of student leaders who serve their school every single day.

"A symbol of courage, responsibility, and the enduring legacy of Dharmapala Vidyalaya."

More than a celebration, ROAR is a torch passed from one generation of Palians to the next — a reminder that true leadership is built on service, and that every roar carries the weight of a proud tradition.

Vision & Mission

What Drives the Roar

Vision

Leaders Who Serve First

To cultivate a generation of Palians who lead with humility, integrity and courage — carrying the values of Dharmapala Vidyalaya far beyond the school gates.

Mission

Honour, Inspire, Unite

To honour the service of the current Prefects' Guild, inspire juniors to embrace responsibility, and unite the school community in celebrating a shared legacy of discipline and pride.
