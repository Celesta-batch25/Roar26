A NEW GENERATIONLEADS WITH PRIDE

Every year, a new circle of student leaders steps forward to carry the weight of a tradition older than most of them realise. Not through titles alone, but through quiet, everyday service — in the corridors, on the field, and in the moments no one is watching.


ROAR is the Annual Prefects' Day of Dharmapala Vidyalaya, Pannipitiya — organised entirely by the Prefects' Guild — where that service is finally seen, celebrated, and passed on. A proud legacy. A golden lion's roar.

Guild Protocol

Rules & Regulations

A few essentials every guest should know before stepping through the gates on the day.

01

Registration Required

Every attendee — including current prefects — must complete online registration to receive an entry pass.

02

QR Entry Only

Entry is granted only on presentation of your QR confirmation, digital or printed, at the gate.

03

Formal Attire

Formal dress is expected. Prefects must wear the official Guild uniform unless notified otherwise.

04

Arrival Time

Please arrive by 4:00 PM. Seating is not guaranteed for guests arriving after the ceremony begins.

05

Respect the Guild

Follow the instructions of Guild officials and event marshals throughout the evening.

06

Photography Consent

The event will be photographed and filmed. Attending implies consent to appear in official ROAR media.

07

No Outside Items

Outside food, banners, or decorations are not permitted on the premises without prior Guild approval.

08

Schedule Changes

The organizing committee reserves the right to amend the programme or venue where necessary.
